5. **Clinical Decision Support**: AI supports clinical decision-making through real-time data analysis, providing healthcare professionals with insights that can lead to better patient outcomes. 6. **Remote Monitoring and Telehealth**: AI technologies are integral to remote monitoring systems and telehealth services, enabling continuous patient engagement and care delivery, particularly in underserved areas.
